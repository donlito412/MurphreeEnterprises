Who We Are

A Consulting Firm Built for the Federal Marketplace — and Beyond

Murphree Enterprises LLC is a Pittsburgh-based, certified small and minority-owned consulting firm that bridges the gap between clients and results. We advise, connect, and manage — so you're always working with the right expertise, the right vendors, and the right strategy.

"We don't just give advice — we get in the room, connect the right people, and make sure the work gets done."

We serve federal agencies and prime contractors looking for a certified consulting sub, and we're equally focused on private sector and commercial clients who need expert guidance on AI adoption, construction project advisory, or procurement strategy. If you need it done right and done compliantly, we're your firm.
